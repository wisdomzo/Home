电波接收功率推测模型

Received Signal Strength Prediction Models

本模型提出一种地理知识驱动、迁移学习增强的 RSS 预测方法。利用开放数据库中的地理信息（经纬度、海拔、建筑高度、土地利用）量化环境特征，采用二维卷积神经网络捕捉环境与 RSS 的复杂关系，提升跨场景泛化能力。引入迁移学习，仅需少量标注样本即可适配新环境，显著降低数据成本。

This model presents a geographical knowledge-driven, transfer learning-enhanced RSS prediction method. Using open geographic data (longitude, latitude, altitude, building heights, land use) to quantify environmental characteristics, a 2D CNN captures complex environment-RSS relationships for improved cross-scenario generalization. Transfer learning enables rapid adaptation with minimal labeled samples, significantly reducing data collection costs.
